Used to select the type of parking facility or structure.

  • Carports — Structures used to offer limited protection to vehicles, primarily cars, from the elements.
  • Multi-story — A multiple level/story parking garage.
  • Sheds — Private hangars for vehicles, located close to owner's home. Usually constructed of profiled metal.
  • Ground Level — Surface parking area or lot.
  • Underground — Parking structures beneath the ground level or underneath buildings.
