A telephone number associated with a point of interest.

Should be entered with spaces, or with hyphens, and contain the country code and area code:

  • +[country code] [area code] [local number]
  • +[country code]-[area code]-[local number]

Verify that you are not violating data privacy laws in your country (especially for private numbers).

Usage Examples

  • +1-727-555-1234
  • +44 207 420 6204
